eLiberate

The online deliberation tool for all.

Sign up now to start deliberating!

eLiberate allows groups to hold online meetings using Roberts Rules of Order even if their members are in different locations. It is nearly ready to be tested with real-world activities. Let us know if your organization is interested in being involved with these efforts. Our current plan is to make eLiberate freely available for online meetings in the near future and to release the software under a free software license at some point in the not-too-distant future. For more information, see the Extended About page.
